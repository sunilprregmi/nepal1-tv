About Us

Nepal1 TV is a leading Nepali-language satellite television channel, widely accessible on platforms such as JioTV, YuppTV, Tata Play, and others. With availability on prominent satellites like SES 7, G-Sat 15, Measat 3b, ST 2, G-Sat 24, and G-Sat 30, Nepal1 TV reaches viewers across Nepal and Indian regions such as North Bengal, Sikkim, the North-East, and parts of Uttar Pradesh. The channel's extensive presence also spans beyond India, connecting Nepalese diaspora communities in places like Burma, Malaysia, and Hong Kong.

Nepal1 TV offers a diverse programming lineup, tailored to serve the informational and entertainment needs of the global Nepali-speaking audience. The channel's content includes everything from news and current affairs to entertainment, music, and spiritual discourses. Popular shows include Superhit and N 1 Songs, which offer a compilation of melodious hits, and Khotal Khatal, a special report focusing on current issues. The channel also features celebrity interviews, fitness and beauty segments, spiritual talks from Sant Rampal and Satpal Maharaj, and live news updates on regional and national happenings.

Nepal1 TV bridges the gap between Nepali media and the global Nepali diaspora by curating content that reflects their interests. Whether it's the latest developments in Nepalese politics or spiritual insights from religious leaders, the channel ensures a wide reach. Additionally, the mix of content in Nepali and Hindi helps it engage a broader audience, positioning Nepal1 TV as a unifying platform for advertisers to tap into a vibrant, diverse consumer base across Nepal, the Indian subcontinent, and beyond.
